In this tutorial, we will take our remote sensing pipeline one step further: Neural Networks on the Edge.

We will fetch Sentinel-2 imagery via `geotiff.js` just like we did for NDVI, but instead of a simple math shader, we will feed the pixels into an INT8 Quantized YOLO model using WebGPU `onnxruntime-web`.

### Why ONNX and WebGPU?
Exporting our PyTorch models to ONNX allows us to leverage cross-platform execution. Combined with WebGPU, the browser has near-native access to the underlying graphics card, enabling inference latencies under 50ms for lightweight detection models.
